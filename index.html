Introduction

We believe technology should serve people.

We're a small studio dedicated to crafting thoughtful, intuitive software that enhances the human experience. Whether it's a mobile app that helps you stay focused, a tool that simplifies everyday tasks, or an interface that brings delight to your routine, our goal is to make technology feel like an extension of you.

What we stand for

  • Human-Centered Design Our software starts with empathy. We prioritize clarity, accessibility, and joy in every product we make.
  • Simplicity with Depth We aim for interfaces that are easy to use right away, but powerful enough to grow with you.
  • Sustainable Craft We build lean, elegant tools that respect your time, attention, and privacy.

Privacy

Effective date: August 1, 2025

Sliver Craftworks LLC (“we”, “us”, or “our”) does not collect, use, store, or share any personal data through our mobile applications or website.

Data Collection
We do not collect any personal information from users. This includes but is not limited to: names, email addresses, device identifiers, usage data, or location data.

Tracking & Analytics
Our apps and website do not use analytics, advertising identifiers, or cookies. We do not track users in any way.

Third-Party Access
No data is shared with third parties, because we do not collect any data to begin with.

Children's Privacy
Our apps are not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from any users, including children.

Changes to This Policy
If our data practices change in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and revise the effective date.
